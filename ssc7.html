SSC MTS 2020

Staff Selection Commission Multitasking Staff Or SSC MTS exam is a non-technical examination conducted by SSC. The recruitment notification for SSC MTS exam 2020 released on 5th February 2021.

SSC MTS Recruitment

SSC MTS Recruitment

SSC MTS Exam is conducted for the following posts:

  • Peon
  • Gardner
  • Daftary
  • Jamadar
  • Junior Gestetner Operator
  • Chowkidar
  • Safaiwala, etc

The SSC releases the SSC MTS Vacancies through an official circular. The vacancy for SSC MTS 2020 will be released once the notification for the same is out. The Staff Selection Commission released a total of 7099 vacancies for SSC MTS 2019.

SSC MTS Salary

The Salary of SSC MTS is revised after the 7th Pay Commission Application. Currently, the in-hand SSC MTS salary is approximately 18,000/ to 22,000/ per month.
